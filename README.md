# FCash 💸 — Personal Expense Management Made Simple

**FCash** is a modern personal finance and expense tracking application designed to help you monitor **income, expenses, savings, and spending habits** in one intuitive dashboard.

Built for clarity and efficiency, FCash makes it easy to understand where your money goes with **visual analytics, transaction history, and Excel (.xlsx) integration**.

---

## Why FCash?

Whether you want to track monthly savings, analyze spending categories, or separate recurring costs from one-time purchases, FCash gives you a clear view of your finances with a sleek dark-themed interface.

Perfect for anyone looking for a:

- **Personal expense manager**
- **Income and savings tracker**
- **Budget monitoring app**
- **Financial dashboard**
- **Excel-based expense analysis tool**

---

## Key Features

### Financial Dashboard
Get a complete overview of your:

- **Income (Stipendi)**
- **Expenses (Spese)**
- **Total Savings (Risparmio)**

all at a glance.

### Excel Integration
Import and manage financial data easily through **`.xlsx` files**.

- Upload spreadsheets
- Replace existing records
- Track transactions directly from Excel data

### Detailed Transaction History
Review your financial activity in a clean chronological timeline with:

- Category-specific icons
- Color-coded entries
- Easy-to-scan expense records

### Ordinary vs Extraordinary Expenses
Differentiate between:

- **Ordinary expenses** — recurring, essential costs
- **Extraordinary expenses** — one-time or non-recurring spending

This helps you understand your real budget flexibility.

## Advanced Analytics

FCash includes powerful finance analytics to help you make better money decisions.

### Monthly Savings Trends
Track how your savings change month over month with visual bar charts.

### Category Composition
Analyze how your spending mix evolves over time, including categories such as:

- Fuel
- Train / Commuting
- Gifts
- Leisure / Going out
- Clothing
- Specific items

### Spending Breakdown
Use donut charts to compare:

- Fixed costs
- Variable costs

### Top Spending Categories
Identify the categories that impact your budget the most with ranked visual charts.

## Data Insights

The **Analysis (Analisi)** section provides a deeper view of your financial health.

### Automatic Savings Calculation
Savings are calculated automatically as:

**Income - Expenses**

### Category Analysis
Track and evaluate spending across key categories, including:

- Train / Commuting
- Fuel
- Gifts
- Leisure / Going out
- Clothing / Specific items

This makes it easier to spot patterns, reduce unnecessary spending, and improve your budget strategy.
